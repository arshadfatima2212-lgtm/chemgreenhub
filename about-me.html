My name is Fatima, and I am a pre-engineering student with a deep interest in Chemical Engineering and Environmental Sustainability. I founded ChemGreen Hub as a student-led platform to share knowledge and guide students on chemical and environmental engineering concepts, with a focus on sustainability and real-world applications.

Recently, I completed a United Nations UNECE e-learning course on Air Pollution Monitoring and Climate Action. This inspired me to create educational content on air quality, sustainable practices, and the role of engineers in protecting the environment. My website is a space where students can learn, share, and apply these ideas.

Beyond academics, I am passionate about learning new skills, including web development, and using technology to support education and environmental awareness. I also enjoy mentoring peers and sharing insights from my learning experiences.

My future goal is to pursue higher education in Chemical Engineering and contribute to sustainable industrial solutions that benefit society and protect the environment. Through ChemGreen Hub, I aim to inspire more students to combine engineering knowledge with environmental responsibility.
